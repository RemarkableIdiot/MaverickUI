About Us

About Maverick

Since 2000, Maverick has provided tens of thousands of businesses with end-to-end, technology-driven payment processing supplemented by our 24/7/365 support, value-added products and competitive rates.

Maverick is a full-service payment processor, which means all operations are performed in-house. This includes partner and merchant support, underwriting and on-boarding, risk and compliance monitoring and more. As a partner, you can fully leverage our operations to focus on what you do best, which is board new merchants. With an industry-leading dashboard that is feature-rich and both merchant and partner facing, we make it easy to scale in a frictionless way while providing emmense value.

Experience

Since 2000, Maverick has provided thousands of merchants of all sizes and in different industries reliable and secure processing systems. As a family-owned company, Maverick’s management shares over 35+ years of experience in the processing space.

Values

At Maverick, we are committed to a sustainable future and to improving the social, economic, and environmental well-being of our community. Furthermore, we work hard to minimize our environmental footprint by using green supplies and engaging in sustainable operations.

Technology

With actionable data & insights, easy-to-use reporting & analytics, advanced fraud prevention & chargeback management, multi-account & location toggling, we have you covered. Our proprietary dashboard provides merchants and partners unmatched business management technology.

Corporate Leadership

Alan is a veteran within the acquiring and payment processing spaces. With extensive knowledge of the industry, Alan is responsible for Maverick’s executive leadership. Prior to founding Maverick, Alan co-founded and sold another large payment processing company. With vast hands-on experience, running day-to-day operations, sales acumen, and entrepreneurial spirit, Alan is a leader and a mentor to everyone on the team.

Having grown up within the payments space, Ben has been with Maverick since its inception. Starting in sales, and working his way up through business development, operations, and executive management, Ben has been a catalyst and evangelist for innovation at Maverick and within the payments industry. Working with his father Alan, Ben grew the business while studying International Financial Management at the University of Southern California. Upon graduation from USC (go Trojans!) Ben joined the executive management team as Chief Operating Officer.

Like his brother Ben, Moe worked his way through the company, gaining insight and experience in sales and operations. Having joined Maverick after his successful graduation from USC with a degree in Business and Economics, Moe quickly found himself drawn to the financial elements of the business. As Chief Revenue Officer, a certified Payments Professional, and board member of the Electronic Transactions Association, Moe is an emerging leader within the industry and always willing to assist fellow team members.
